Q1

What exactly do I get in the Free School Audit?

+

A 30-minute call where we review your school's current social presence, ad spend, and admissions funnel — then send a written diagnostic with 3 specific fixes you can action whether you work with us or not.

Q2

We've worked with agencies before — they didn't move the needle. Why is TCP different?

+

We don't sell 'content packages.' We're hired to move admissions numbers. Every reel, every ad, every parent-facing video maps to a measurable outcome — applications, walk-ins, or cost-per-lead — agreed before we shoot a frame.

Q3

Do you understand the local Jaipur / Rajasthan school market?

+

Yes. We're based in Jaipur and have shot inside K-12 campuses across the city. We know the parent-decision cycle here, what reads as 'premium' to a Rajasthani parent vs. a transferred-in family, and which platforms actually drive admission walk-ins locally.

Q4

How long until we see results from the campaigns?

+

Brand-recall lift in 30 days. Cost-per-lead reduction by week 6. Admission-quality leads in the funnel by month 2 — assuming a typical mid-cycle launch. We share a weekly dashboard so you're never guessing.

Q5

What does a typical engagement cost?

+

Engagements start at the equivalent of one extra admission per month. We scope per-school after the audit — pricing depends on shoot days, ad spend, and campaign length. No retainer lock-ins.
